About Us

We’re Elias Kgaphola and Mashoto Matsi, the brains behind Affordable Rooms.

Our mission? To make finding a room easy, especially for students. We saw how tough it was to go door-to-door hunting for a place to stay, so we built this platform to bring the search to your fingertips.

Whether you’re a student on a budget or just someone needing a comfy spot, we’ve got you covered. No stress, no hassle—just affordable rooms in your comfort zone.
