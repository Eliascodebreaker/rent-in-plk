Who We Are

We’re Elias Kgaphola and Mashoto Matsi, the duo behind Rent In PLK.

It all started with a simple idea: help students find rooms without the hassle of going door-to-door. We’ve been there—knocking on doors, stressing about budgets—so we built this platform to make it easy, affordable, and stress-free.

Our focus is on students, but we’re here for anyone needing a room that fits their life. Comfort zone searching, all the way!
